Art Competition "Opening"




Dates of the event: from March 29, 2020 onwards
Deadline to submit works: April 30, 2020 
Opening of online exhibition: April 31, 2020

The new online exhibition of Artli Gallery will be based on the competition themed "Opening". Waiting on the eve of new opportunities, immediate impressions and fulfillment from the desires and hopes coming true. This is the vision of discovery by Arti Gallery founders, but there are no limits for artists for interpretation on their own. All artists and photographers over 18 years old are invited to participate in the competition. Accepting 2D works performed in any technique.

RULES OF CONTESTS
 There are general rules for our competitions. The agreement contains the following conditions. Please read our terms and procedures carefully before making the submissions. Competitions will have different subjects, and it is very important to follow a specific topic.
- We invite all artists from around the world
- Artists must be 18 years or older to apply
- Submissions must be relevant to the subject of the competition
- Works with pornographic or satanic content, involving political or religious insights will not be taken into consideration
- 3 images may be submitted for free.
- Illustrations should be marked as: First Name, Last Name, Artwork Title, jpg
- Image size must be up to 1.5 MB in JPG format only
- The artwork image should be visible, clear and focused. Please crop any background in the images.
- Images must be original work created by the artist
- Artli Gallery provides you with the opportunity to contact potential buyers.
HOW TO APPLY
Your name
Country

Website

Artist statement (up to 300 words)
Title of Artwork

Select 3 images (up to 1.5 MB) of your art work.
Send an application to artligallery2020@gmail.com

ELIGIBILITY
This is an international competition for all the artists above 18 years. All means of visual art are acceptable as 2D image (painting, drawing, photography, sculpture, digital printing, printing, decoration, collage or installation), except video and sound art.

SELF-IMAGES
- Illustrations should be marked as: First Name, Last Name, Artwork Title.jpg
- Image size must be up to 1.5 MB in JPG format.
- Only artwork should be visible, clear and focused. Please crop any background in the images.
- Images must be original works created by the artist.

JUDGING CRITERIA
The following evaluation criteria will be used to evaluate artwork: creativity, interpretation of the theme, originality and quality of art, overall design, demonstration of artistic abilities and inspiration from the environment.

AWARDS
All the winning artists will receive a digital diploma, the first, second and third places will be largely displayed with an article about the artist and his work on gallery website and social media. The winning work (first place) will be on the poster of the show.
All accepted works will be displayed with the name of the artist, the title and details of the artwork and a link to their website or email address/social media page if they do not have a website. All accepted artists will receive extensive online advertising and promotion on our website.

RIGHTS TO USE
This Agreement constitutes the terms between the artist and the Artli Gallery. Before submitting their works, all artists who want to take part in any competition on our website Artli Gallery must agree to the following:
- Artli Gallery will use your images only for marketing, advertising and promotion purposes for any competition on our website.
- You, the artist, agree to allow the Artli Gallery to archive selected works of art as part of the exhibition. It will be available for public viewing on our website Artli Gallery, instagram and facebook.
- Selected works will include the name of the artist, the title of the art work and a link to their website or email address/social media page if they do not have a website.
- The artist retains all copyrights to any image that they represent in the Artli Gallery.
